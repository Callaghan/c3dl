C3DL Orbit Camera (pitch and yaw) Test

This page tests the pitch and yaw capabilities of OrbitCamera Click and drag with the mouse to use these capabilities. Vertical movement will pitch the camera, horizontal movement will yaw the camera around it's current 'up' axis.



Click and drag or use the keyboard keys along with the
mouse wheel to orbit about the object.

Z + mouse wheel = zoom in/out
P + mouse wheel = pitch
Y + mouse wheel = yaw
