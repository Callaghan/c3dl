C3DL Orbit Camera (roll) Test

This page tests the roll capabilities of OrbitCamera Click and drag with the mouse to use these capabilities. Vertical movement will roll the camera, while horizontal movement will roll the camera around the camera's up axis.



Click and drag or use the keyboard keys along with the
mouse wheel to orbit about the object.

Z + mouse wheel = zoom in/out
P + mouse wheel = roll
Y + mouse wheel = yaw
