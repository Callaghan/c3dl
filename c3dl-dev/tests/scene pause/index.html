C3DL Pause Test

This test will show and test the PauseScene, PauseRender, and PauseUpdate functions. When the Render is paused the objects in the scene will still be updating but not redrawn. To test pause and unpause render and the duck will move as if it was rotating the entire time and added objects will not be shown. When the Update is paused the objects in the scene will not be updating but the scene will still be drawn. To test pause update and click add duck the new duck will be added but neither will update their rotation. When the Scene is paused the objects in the scene will not be updating or redrawn. To test pause scene and click add duck and the new duck will be added but not drawn and neither will update their rotation.
